Miscellaneous

For video editing, In my workflow, I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro to assemble footages and arrange shots into a cohesive sequence. Premiere's robust toolkit helps me to refine the timing of cuts, apply subtle transitions like cross dissolves for continuity, and overlay custom graphics, titles, and lower thirds designed in Adobe Illustrator. For color correction and additional visual effects, I often turn to Adobe Photoshop to utilize filters like Lumetri Color to enhance mood or apply particle effects for ambiance. I also sample music beds and foley effects, carefully adjusting levels with keyframes in Premiere's audio mixer. Overall, the goal is to employ the editing tools at my disposal to craft an immersive video that resonates with viewers.
